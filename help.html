Orders & Shipping

You can track your order by:

  • Going to "Your Orders" in your account
  • Clicking "Track Package" next to your order
  • Using the tracking number provided in your shipping confirmation email
  • Checking your mobile app for real-time updates

We offer several shipping options:

  • Standard Delivery: FREE on orders over $35 (3-5 business days)
  • Express Delivery: $9.99 (1-2 business days)
  • Same-Day Delivery: $19.99 (available in select cities)
  • Prime FREE Same-Day: Free for Prime members in eligible areas

You can modify or cancel your order if it hasn't been shipped yet:

  • Go to "Your Orders" and find the order
  • If the order shows "Not yet shipped," you can cancel or modify it
  • Once shipped, you can return the items after delivery
  • Some digital products cannot be cancelled once downloaded

Returns & Refunds

To return an item:

  • Go to "Your Orders" and find the item
  • Click "Return or Replace Items"
  • Select the reason for return
  • Choose return method (drop-off or pickup)
  • Print the return label and ship the item back

Our return policy includes:

  • 30-day return window for most items
  • Items must be in original condition
  • Free returns on most items
  • Extended holiday return period (Nov-Jan)
  • Some items (perishables, personalized items) cannot be returned

Refund timeline:

  • Credit/Debit cards: 3-5 business days after we receive your return
  • Gift cards: Instant refund after return is processed
  • Bank transfers: 3-10 business days
  • Original payment method will be credited

Your Account

If you're having trouble signing in:

  • Check if you're using the correct email and password
  • Try resetting your password
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Disable browser extensions temporarily
  • Contact customer service if the problem persists

To update your account:

  • Go to "Your Account" page
  • Click "Login & Security" to change email or password
  • Click "Your Addresses" to manage shipping addresses
  • Click "Your Payments" to update payment methods
  • Changes are saved automatically

To delete your account:

  • Go to "Your Account" page
  • Scroll to the bottom and click "Close Your Account"
  • Follow the prompts to confirm account closure
  • Note: This action cannot be undone
  • Outstanding orders and subscriptions will be processed
